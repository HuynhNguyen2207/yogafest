What is yogafest?

Yogafest 2017 is the start of the annual festival that celebrate healthy lifestyle, while raising environmental awareness.

What to do there?

Practice Outdoor Yoga

Enjoy the fresh air and sun while joining our outdoor Yoga sections instructed by cerfified gurus.

Listen to Great Music

The festival feature a wide variety of indie band. After Yoga, sit down and enjoy the music.

Enjoy good food

At Serpentine Bar & Kitchen - a green heart of London in Hyde Park for meal with friends, family and kids. Also with BBQ menu with fresh meat and pizza

Help the environment

During the festival, we encourauge you to use only recyclable items, such as bring your own tents for camping.
